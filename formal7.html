Conclusion

Overall, because marketing is one of the most vital things to a business, there are many things that come together to make sure the marketing plan goes well and is effective. Some of the most important of these things that may be overlooked every now and then are research, communication, and technology. While it seems like marketing may not be too difficult on the surface, there is a lot of action going on behind the scenes that isn’t always so obvious but is very important nonetheless. The research part of marketing is very important because if a business doesn’t know anything about their target market then there’s almost no use trying to market something to them. Communication is also key because the way something is presented can change the entire view on it, whether it is communication between a buiness and it’s customers or another business. So communication becomes crucial for getting the point across about a certain product in exactly the way a business wants it. And finally, technology plays many different roles in marketing, some more apparent than others, that are all vital to a businesses marketing efforts. These roles ranging from using technology to broadcast a commercial or post on social media, to using cookies to track information and store it in a database so as to learn more about customers and market to their specific needs. Among the many comonents that make up marketing and all of the things involved with it, research, communication, and technology all play very important roles when it comes to marketing.
