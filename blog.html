Video Analysis

  • Purpose of the video:

    I think the purpose of this video was for the author to express his feelings about his uncles death and being gay. I also this he was using it as a way to cope with such a traumatic event, and give hope to others out there that may be in the same sort of situation as him and show people that there's always another choice and that things will get better.

  • How the video attempts achieve it's purpose:

    This video attempts to achieve its purpose, I think mainly by being so personal and emotional. You can hear in his voice how important this issue is to him and It helps viewers to relate to it even if they haven’t experienced a similar situation because you can feel the emotion in it and you feel sympathy for him. Also I think because of the emotion in it helps to inspire other people in the same way and help give them inspiration.

  • Structure of the message:

    The structure of this message was mainly a personal letter that he wrote to his dead unlce who experienced a similar situation to what he was experiencing at the time, but he made it more personal by reading the letter out loud. He probably wanted to make it into a video so that people from all across the country that may share similar stories can relate to him

  • How the video's structure can help revise the Lived Experiences Project

    Because this video is so emotional and personal I think it can help me to revise my project into being more personal. Detroit Commercial

I think this commercial is very successful because of the different methods they used. The imagery of Detroit helped a lot because it shows the viewers exactly where the cars are coming from and makes them feel more connected to the car and the whole commercial. I also think the voiceover was very important to the effectiveness of the commercial because the man is using the ethos appeal by saying things like "what does a town that's been to hell and back know about the finer things in life?" but then saying "its the hottest fires that make the hardest steel" it's very convincing and is trying to show that Detroit may have been trough a lot but the one thing they really know how to do well is make cars. One of the last things that really helped make the commercial as effective as it was was having "Lose Yourself" by eminem playing in the background. This was effective not only because Eminem himself was featured in the commercial but because it is a well known song and the meaning the song has that can be related back to Detroit. The song itself is a lot about seizing an opportunity to be successful which is a lot similar to what Detroit has done. Specifically the song says things like "If you had one shot, or one opportunity To seize everything you ever wanted. one moment, would you capture it or just let it slip?" which is could be seen as a refernce to how the motor industry is a lot like Detroits one opportunity that they captured to be successful and didn't just let it pass by. Another line in the song that seems very relevant is "Success is my only motherf*cking option, failure's not." This line is similar to how Detroit has pushed trough everything and come through all of their struggles and come out on top to be known as the motor city and make some of the best cars in the world.
