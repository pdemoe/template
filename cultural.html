Modern Family: The Debate Against Same-Sex Marriage

“With liberty and justice for all.” Sounds familiar doesn’t it? That’s because this is a part of our pledge of allegiance. A part of something that we as Americans promise to each other, yet somehow there are still people out there who don’t experience the same liberty and justice as everyone else. These people are the members of the gay community and although they are really no different than anyone else, they are treated with less respect. In this day and age, however, much of the younger generations are heavily influenced by popular media such as TV shows like the popular comedy show Modern Family. This show features two main characters that are in an openly gay relationship that have an adopted Vietnamese daughter. Shows like this can have a huge impact on the viewers and it is a good thing that there are such shows because of the limited rights that same-sex couples currently have. Same-sex couples should have the same rights as everyone else in this country so by promoting and increasing shows with openly gay characters that are portrayed in a positive light, hopefully the public opinion on gay marriage will improve and it will soon become 100% legalized.

Love. Such a simple word yet it has so much meaning. Some people spend their entire life trying to find what we call love which, admittedly, is not easy to come by. Those who are lucky enough to find true love usually want to get married to make it an official promise, but unfortunately for a select group of people, this is not possible for them to do in most countries on earth. Once they find true love they aren’t allowed to have the simple pleasure of being happy and marrying the person they want to be with for the rest of their life. They are suddenly not worthy of marriage or become viewed as lesser to society, and all of this is only because some other people, who may not even know them, have decided that they’re not comfortable with these people getting married and that they shouldn’t be allowed to have the happy ending that they want. These people are subject to what is essentially just bullying simply because their lifestyle doesn’t fit what everybody’s ideal lifestyle is, and it’s time that this ends. This issue is the debate on same-sex marriage, which has become an increasingly popular topic in society today and even more so in the media. Many shows nowadays, such as the show Modern Family, feature gay couples and this can have a huge impact on the same-sex marriage issue. In fact, there was a study conducted by Ipsos MediaCT that showed that about 18% of people aged 13-64 that watch TV say that television in general has changed their opinion of same-sex marriage in a positive way.
